Central Training & Placement Cell

The Central Training & Placement Cell (CTPC) to cater to the needs of leading organisations in conducting campus interviews for the final year students. It is headed by a Training and Placement officers and supported by department placement coordinators. The cell is assisted by student representatives from various branches of study. The placement cell provides the best arrangements and hospitality to the HR team from various companies. .
